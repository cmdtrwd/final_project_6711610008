Chapter 01 — The Garden

It Started With a Garden.

In 2018, our founders converted a half-acre plot in the Pacific Northwest into an experimental botanical garden. The goal was simple: grow ingredients worthy of a premium beverage.

Three seasons later, the first batch of Alpine Mist rolled out of a small rented kitchen. The response was overwhelming.

Read the full origin story

Chapter 02 — The Philosophy

Minimal Ingredients. Maximum Intention.

Every ION flavor is built around a single botanical hero — one primary ingredient sourced from a specific region. Everything else exists only to elevate it.

We don't add artificial flavors, sweeteners, or preservatives. We don't need to.

WHAT WE STAND FOR

Three principles that guide every decision we make — from sourcing to shipping.

01

Radical Transparency

Every ingredient, every supplier, and every carbon offset is published publicly on our website. No hiding, ever.

02

Terroir-Driven

We travel to the source. Our rose petals come from Grasse, France. Our juniper from Montana. Place matters.

03

Zero Waste

100% aluminum packaging. Spent botanicals composted. Our manufacturing facility runs on renewable energy.
